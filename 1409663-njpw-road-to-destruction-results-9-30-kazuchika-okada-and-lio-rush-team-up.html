NJPW Road To Destruction Results (9/30): Kazuchika Okada And Lio Rush Team Up

Publish date: 2024-08-06

On September 30, New Japan Pro Wrestling continued its Road to Destruction tour with the fifteenth night of action from Korakuen Hall in Tokyo, Japan.

In the main event, Kazuchika Okada and Lio Rish teamed up to face Tetsuya Naito and Hiromu Takahashi.

The results, courtesy of Fightful, are as follows:

  • HENARE defeated Oskar Leube
  • Hiroshi Tanahashi & CHAOS (Tomohiro Ishii & YOSHI-HASHI) defeated Tiger Mask, Oleg Boltin & Yuto Nakashima
  • Los Ingobernables de Japon (Shingo Takagi, Yota Tsuji & BUSHI) defeated United Empire (Jeff Cobb, Great-O-Khan & Callum Newman)
  • Guerrillas Of Destiny (Hikuleo & El Phantasmo) & Intergalactic Jet Setters (KUSHIDA & Kevin Knight) defeated BULLET CLUB War Dogs (Drilla Moloney, Gabe Kidd, Alex Coughlin & Clark Connors)
  • Guerrillas Of Destiny (Tama Tonga, Tanga Loa & Jado) defeated BULLET CLUB (David Finlay, Chase Owens & Gedo)
  • : Strong Style Minoru Suzuki, (El Desperado & Ren Narita) defeated Shota Umino, Yuji Nagata & Master Wato
  • Just 4 Guys (SANADA, DOUKI & TAKA Michinoku) defeated House Of Torture (SHO, EVIL & Yoshinobu Kanemaru)
  • CHAOS (Kazuchika Okada & Lio Rush) defeated Los Ingobernables de Japon (Tetsuya Naito & Hiromu Takahashi)
