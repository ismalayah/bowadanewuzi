Tisto And The Chainsmokers Finally Revisit Split (Only U)

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Tiësto and The Chainsmokers have been teasing a collaboration for the better part of the year, and it looks like it’s finally got a release date. The Dutch music icon brought the New York City-based duo onstage during his set at Ultra Europe to debut the the track last month, but has only just now uploaded a high-quality snippet to the Musical Freedom Recordings SoundCloud account.

Recommended Videos

Undoubtedly named after the island in Croatia where Ultra Europe took place, “Split (Only U)” blends the monster synths prevalent in most of Tiësto’s recent discography with the trappy 808 drum rolls characteristic of The Chainsmokers’ creative vein of late. Unfortunately, as much as we were looking forward to the release, the influences do clash a bit with one another, resulting in an obvious collaboration that fails to measure up to the capabilities of either outfit.

Regardless, “Split (Only U)” by Tiësto and The Chainsmokers will come out through Musical Freedom Recordings on August 31st.
