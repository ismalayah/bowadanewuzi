New 3 From Hell Photo Sees Captain Spaulding In Prison

Publish date: 2024-06-15

You’ve gotta love Rob Zombie.

Recommended Videos

After telling us just yesterday that his highly anticipated sequel to The Devil’s Rejects, 3 From Hell, won’t be ready until next year, the director’s been making the wait easier on fans by giving them juicy looks at the film. First, it was two photos that showed us Baby and Otis, who were apparently in court and on trial for their crimes.

Now, Zombie’s back to deliver even more goodies and this time, he’s shared a shot of Captain Spaulding, who’s in prison and behind bars. But that’s not all, as the beloved filmmaker has also brought us another pic, which focuses on Jeff Daniel Phillips, who’s apparently playing Virgil Dallas Harper, the prison’s warden that’ll have to deal with Spaulding and the others.

Though neither photo is terribly revealing, it’s still great to get a look at the threequel regardless and you can see both of them in the gallery below – along with the two that Zombie brought us yesterday.

That’s all we’ve got for now, unfortunately, but you can bet that there’s more photos where these came from and at the rate the director’s been sharing them, we may even get one or two more before the week’s out. Until then, though, fans can rest easy knowing that they’re in for one heck of a good time with 3 From Hell, as star Bill Moseley recently promised us that the threequel will blow our minds.

Also starring Austin Stoker (Assault on Precinct 13), Dee Wallace (The Howling, Cujo), Dot-Marie Jones (American Horror Story) and Tom Papa, there’s still no word of a concrete release date for the pic, but fans of Rob Zombie’s eccentric oeuvre can go on enjoying updates from the Firefly Family, safe in the knowledge that 3 From Hell is coming together quite nicely.
