Olan Prenatt Biography, Age, Height, Girlfriend, Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Olan Prenatt is a skateboarder and an actor in Hollywood. Prenatt is a rising star in the entertainment industry. He has a charismatic personality and versatile talent. Prenatt is best known for his role as Fuckshit in the 2018 coming-of-age film Mid90s, directed by Jonah Hill. He is also set to star in the upcoming thriller Good Girl Jane, alongside Bella Thorne and Jake Manley.

Early Life

In Los Angeles, California, Olan Prenatt was born on September 18, 1996. He has mixed ethnicity, with African American ancestry from his mother’s side. His mother, Tanya Neely, is a professional coach who helped him develop his confidence and communication skills. And his brother Andrew is his younger sibling.

Olan grew up in a skateboarding culture and developed a passion for the sport at a young age. Prenatt was a wide receiver for the football team at Hamilton High School. On his Instagram account, he began sharing videos of himself skating, which caught the eye of advertisers and followers.

Olan Prenatt Career

Olan started as a skateboarder and took off when he joined the Illegal Civilization Skateboard Team, a group of young skaters who produce videos and merchandise. Prenatt was featured in their promo video Chestnuts Roasting, which showcased his skills and style. He also worked with other skateboarding teams and brands, such as Arbor, Gnarly, and Rogue Status.

Aside from skateboarding, Olan also pursued a career as a model and actor. Popular companies like Jordan’s, Adidas, Nike, and Nautica Boys are among the ones he has worked with. Prenatt has also had appearances in several publications, including Paper, Vogue, GQ, Nylon, and Vogue.

Olan’s breakthrough role as an actor came in 2018 when he was cast as Fuckshit in Mid90s, a film that follows a group of teenage skaters in Los Angeles in the 1990s. Prenatt impressed the director Jonah Hill with his natural charisma and authenticity during the audition process.

He received critical acclaim for his performance and was nominated for several awards, such as the Independent Spirit Award for Best Supporting Male. Since then, Olan has appeared in other films and TV shows mainly during 2020.

As per IMDB, he got credited for his work in Godspeed, Unsubscribe, and Nowhere Alaska in 2020. The next year he appeared in Help Wanted, Malicious Motives in 2021. He is also worked in Good Girl Jane, Sick, Mixed Nuts, River of Ghosts, Brunch So Hard, and Melody in 2022.
