Lil Mister Biography – Lil Mister Wiki

Lil Mister born Antwon Fields, was Chicago rapper. He has been known for contributing to the Windy City’s infamous drill scene.

His breakout track was 2012’s “No Lackin.” After serving some time in jail for charges related to speeding and gun possession, he returned to the streets in 2016 and continued to put out music. His other well-known song was “Take Off” which featured Diesel. In February 2019, a month before his death, he dropped the video for his single “Ride or Die.”

In addition to being Lil Durk’s cousin, Lil Mister was a friend of the late Chicago rapper Lil Jojo. In their tribute to Lil Mister, hip-hop blog Kollege Kidd said the rapper was credited with “popularizing the drill movement.”

Lil Mister Age

He was born on December 25, 1995 in Chicago. He was shot and killed on March 15, 2019. He was 24 years old at the time of his death.

Lil Mister Real Name

Lil Mister’s real name is Antwon Fields.

Lil Mister Death

Mister was shot and killed on March 15, 2019 in Chicago. According to Police, the shooting occurred around 7:15 p.m., at the 7400 block of South Harvard Avenue. He was transported to the University of Chicago Medical Center where he was pronounced dead.
