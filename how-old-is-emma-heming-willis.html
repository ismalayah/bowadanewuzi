How old is Emma Heming Willis?

Publish date: 2024-05-02

Emma Heming Willis is Bruce Willis’ second spouse – the pair married in 2009, and have two children together. The Die Hard star was previously married to actor Demi Moore and had three children before their split in the year 2000.

Outside of being the wife of the famous actor, Heming Willis is a model and founded a skincare company called CocoBaba.

Emma Heming Willis’ age

Emma Heming Willis was born on June 18, 1978, making the model 45 years of age. Bruce Willis, meanwhile, celebrated his 68th birthday this year and was born on March 19, 1955.

If you don’t want to do the math, that means the age gap between the couple is 24 years. They welcomed two children into the world a few years after getting married – Mabel Ray in 2012, and Evelyn Penn in 2014.

Emma Heming Willis has kept fans of the action star in the loop about his aphasia over the course of the past year and offered an update that her husband had received a more concrete diagnosis, that being frontotemporal dementia.
