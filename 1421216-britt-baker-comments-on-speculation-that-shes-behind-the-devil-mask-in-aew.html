Britt Baker Comments On Speculation That Shes Behind The Devil Mask In AEW

Publish date: 2024-06-22

Britt Baker has seen your devil speculation all over social media.

All Elite Wrestling‘s Britt Baker was a recent guest on 103.5 KISS FM. When asked about the current online speculation that she’s behind MJF’s devil mask on AEW programming, Baker said she’s seen the speculation, but it isn’t her.

“I’ve seen,” Britt Baker admitted. “I’m not, but then I’m like, maybe I would be a good devil. But it’s not me. But if I could be, would I be? Maybe. Because then I’m like, hmm, who better to stir some shit up than me.”

When Baker asked the radio host who he’d like to be the devil, he said he’d like it to be a surprise. Baker agreed and said that’s the problem with the internet nowadays, with everyone trying to fish for answers.

“You don’t want to know,” Britt Baker said. “But that’s the problem with the internet is they’re all trying to fish and prod. They want to figure everything out, but then when they figure it out, they’re pissed that they figured it out. So you can’t have it all, guys!”
