Mawile (Japanese: クチート Kucheat) is a Hyper-class Tretta. It is part of the Glaciate! Kyurem Assault set.

Tretta data

Pokémon description
It puts opponents off guard with its cute behaviour before turning around chomping and biting.
かわいくふるまって あいてがゆだんしたところで ふりむいてガブリとかみつく。
Additional abilities

This Pokémon can Mega Evolve into Mega Mawile
via use of the Pokémon Mega Ring, Pokémon Mega
Bangle, or in-game Key Stone.

Trivia

Origin

Iron Head and Play Rough are both moves that Mawile can learn in the games.
