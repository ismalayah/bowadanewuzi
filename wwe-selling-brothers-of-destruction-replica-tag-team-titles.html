WWE Selling Brothers Of Destruction Replica Tag Team Titles

Publish date: 2024-07-06

The Undertaker and Kane were great rivals, but better tag team partners as a part of The Brothers of Destruction in WWE. They were even multi-time tag team champions at one point. Now the company is celebrating the two with their own replica tag team champions.

WWE is selling a Signature Series replica WWE Tag Team Championship belt designed for the demons of Death Valley. The design is based on the WWE Tag Team Championship belt that was used from 1985 until 2002. 

The belt features a split design. One-half of the design represents Kane and the other half represents The Undertaker.

The Undertaker and Kane are nearly unstoppable forces on their own, but they leave biblical devastation in their wake when they join forces as The Brothers of Destruction. Commemorate over two decades of The Phenom and The Big Red Monster decimating the WWE landscape with this Signature Series replica title belt. Kane’s Hellfire and The Undertaker’s Deadman imagery meet in the middle of this World Tag Team Championship, making it the perfect dark and disturbing addition to any discerning fan’s WWE collection.

The championship is available for $499.99 on WWEShop.com. It has to be a great collector’s item for any hardcore wrestling fan. You may check out some photos below.
